Purpose of the Website

The Flight Booker website aims to provide users with a convenient platform to search and book flights for their travel needs. Whether it's for business trips, vacations, or any other purpose, our website offers a user-friendly interface to find the perfect flight options.

Basic Features

  • Search for flights by origin and destination cities
  • Select departure and return dates
  • Choose the number of passengers
  • View flight search results
  • Book flights with payment confirmation
